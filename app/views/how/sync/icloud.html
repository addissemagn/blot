iCloud

You can connect your site's folder to Blot with iCloud. You can switch your site to another sync method at any point.

Can Blot access all the files in my iCloud?

No, Blot only has access to your site’s folder.

Can I rename the folder I sync with Blot?

Yes. You can move or rename the folder synced with Blot.

Bandwidth limits

iCloud’s traffic limits do not apply because Blot hosts your folder’s contents on its own servers.

File size limit

Blot can sync individual files up to 100 MB from iCloud. Larger files are not synced.
