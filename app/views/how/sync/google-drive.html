Google Drive

You can connect your site's folder to Blot with Google Drive, which is available on most platforms. You can switch your site to another sync method at any time.

Can Blot access all the files in my Google Drive?

No, Blot only has access to your site’s folder.

Using your phone or tablet

Many text editors for phones and tablets integrate with Google Drive.

Bandwidth limits

Google Drive’s traffic limits do not apply because Blot hosts your folder’s contents on its own servers.

Multiple sites

You can sync as many sites as you want with Google Drive. When you add a site, Blot creates a separate folder for it.
