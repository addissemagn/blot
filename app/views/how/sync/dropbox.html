Dropbox

You can connect your site's folder to Blot with Dropbox, a file hosting service available on most platforms. You can switch your site to another sync method at any point.

Can Blot access all the files in my Dropbox?

No, Blot only requests access to your site’s folder in your Dropbox by default. You can also choose to grant Blot special access to your entire Dropbox. Only do this if you want to move your site’s folder, or share it with other people who use Dropbox.

Bandwidth limits

Dropbox’s traffic limits do not apply because Blot hosts the contents of your folder on its own server.

File size limit

Blot can sync individual files up to 100 MB from Dropbox. Larger files are not synced.

Using your phone or tablet

Many text editors for phones and tablets integrate with Dropbox. Common options include iA Writer. Some customers also use Bear and Ulysses.

Multiple sites

You can create more than one site using Dropbox. When you add a second site, Blot moves the existing site folder into a subfolder of the Dropbox App folder, then creates a second subfolder for the new site.
